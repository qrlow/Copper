    How Scenarios Are Built

    Assumptions, not sourced forecasts

    The same public data baseline is used in every scenario: ICSG 2024 refined usage, USGS MCS 2026 2024 world mine/refinery supply, and World Bank macro drivers.

    The scenarios change only the assumption knobs: demand growth, transition-demand bonus, mine/refinery growth, disruption loss, and scrap/recycling response.

    Bull means stronger demand and tighter supply. Bear means weaker demand and looser supply. Base sits between them.
